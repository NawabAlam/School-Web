About Us

Johnson Secondary School is a K-10 co-educational Day School established in 1982, with a view to offering world-class educational opportunities to its students. The school is located in the area of Herbertpur, Dehradun in Uttarakhand which is sometimes referred to as "Dev Bhoomi". The school is affiliated to CBSE (Central Board of Secondary Education). The School was founded by Mrs. P. Mega along with her husband Mr. D. Mega in the year 1982. At Johnson Secondary School, we are always guided by our philosophy of providing children healthy learning experiences, while inculcating the distinct features of a school of the future to enhance critical thinking, creativity, collaboration, and communication.
